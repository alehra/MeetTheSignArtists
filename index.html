Meet the Sign Artists

by Alexa Maldonado

Here in Queens, NY, at one of the two Trader Joe's within the borough, there are some amazing sign artists. As an artist myself, I wanted to make something informative about a topic I care about. I'm one of the newest, but I work as one of the few sign artists at the Long Island City Trader Joe's and have come to be fascinated by my work colleagues' process, so I have interviewed them and included some of their work to show the variety of styles. Being able to make these signs is a underappreciated skill, and I wanted to know what goes on the minds of just a handful of the artists at my store. Everyone has a personal touch that they bring to their signage despite the guidelines we have.
